# Source Acquisition Backlog: Unilever x Google Cloud Case

## Priority 1: Protagonist and Decision Evidence

- Find at least one full transcript, interview, or event discussion featuring Sam Kini.
- Capture exact phrasing on strategy trade-offs, not only project updates.
- Prefer full text files in sources/transcripts for T1 quality.

## Priority 2: Financial Grounding

- Unilever annual report and latest interim report with digital, marketing, or productivity references.
- Earnings call transcript(s) mentioning technology, cloud, AI, or operating model changes.
- Any investor day material discussing technology ROI, marketing productivity, or supply chain modernization.

## Priority 3: Partnership Details

- Official announcement(s) from Unilever and Google Cloud.
- Follow-up technical implementation writeups (architecture, operating model, governance).
- Evidence of scope boundaries (which geographies, business units, systems).

## Priority 4: Independent Validation

- At least 3 independent sources from FT, Reuters, Bloomberg, WSJ, The Information, or equivalent.
- At least 1 analyst/industry report with CPG digital transformation benchmarks.
- At least 1 competitor comparator (for example P&G, Nestle, PepsiCo) with similar initiatives.

## Minimum Viable Source Pack (to move from RED to YELLOW)

- 2 transcript/interview sources
- 2 investor/financial sources
- 3 independent news sources
- 1 industry report
